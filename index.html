Successfully implementing high-quality wind and grid-connected solar as well as rooftop solar projects, SHAKTI has a promising position in the cleantech industry across the world. We are already a heavyweight in India and Australia with [1.3GW] installed renewables capacity and another [1.5GW] under construction in both the domestic and overseas market.The company has ambitions to operate across Europe, Asia and African markets.

We'll soon be shipping our power analytics engine to the market. This will serve as a energy monitoring platform - helping our customers in poer management. It'll be a one-stop solution coming up with forecasting, monitoring as well as feedback to meet the desired power consumption and management. The power analytics engine will also double as a finanacial ledger, recording the amount of power sent to the grid and money saved during the process.
